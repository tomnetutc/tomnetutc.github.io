    About The Page

    The TOMNET Center's GitHub repository provides access to a range of data, tools, and software products developed by TOMNET researchers. Our center is committed to advancing transportation research and making our findings accessible. We support the open-source community by sharing resources that are crucial for understanding and predicting transportation trends and behaviors. Here, you can find our latest open-source tools and data, which are available for educational and research purposes.
